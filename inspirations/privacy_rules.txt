==================================================
PRIVACY RULES
=============

Friends CAN see:

* Today's Targets
* Today's Progress
* Today's Daily Score
* Total Lifetime Points
* Current Streak

Friends CANNOT see:

* History Calendar
* Previous Days Tasks
* Previous Daily Scores
* Past Completion Data
* Notifications
* Friend Requests
* Personal Account Settings

History remains completely private.

Only today's activity is visible to friends.
