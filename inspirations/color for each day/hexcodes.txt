Use the following completion-state colors throughout the history calendar.

No Targets:
#E8EAE7

0% Completed:
#A40033

1-49% Completed:
#959EC9

50-99% Completed:
#4E5174

100% Completed:
#28264B

These colors represent completion states and should be used consistently in the monthly history calendar.

Do not replace them with generic red/orange/yellow/green colors.

The calendar should feel elegant, premium, and cohesive with the dark productivity-focused design.
