# Momentum Character System

## Overview

Every user has exactly ONE current character.

Characters are dynamic and can change.

Characters should reflect who the user currently is.

Characters are based on behavior, not permanent achievements.

---

# Core Metrics

Calculate these metrics for every user:

## Streak Score

Measures:

* Current streak
* Longest streak
* Missed days

Represents:
Consistency

---

## Completion Score

Measures:

* Tasks completed
* Completion percentage

Represents:
Execution

---

## Focus Score

Measures:

* Pillar dedication
* Long-term goal commitment
* Avoiding constant switching

Represents:
Focus

---

## Balance Score

Measures:

* Performance across all pillars

Represents:
Balance

---

## Legacy Score

Measures:

* Long-term goals completed
* Progress toward goals
* Long-term commitment

Represents:
Dream chasing

---

# Character Assignment

## Gintoki

Assign when:

* Multiple inactive days
* Very low consistency
* Very low completion

---

## Naruto

Assign when:

* Recovering from failure
* Recently restarted progress
* Improvement trend is strong

---

## Tanjiro

Assign when:

* 5+ day streak
* 80%+ completion rate

---

## Shoyo Hinata

Assign when:

* Active regularly
* Improving steadily

---

## Rock Lee

Assign when:

* High workload
* High task completion volume

---

## Shikamaru

Assign when:

* Backlog is cleared
* No overdue tasks
* Efficient execution

---

## Zoro

Assign when:

* Strong focus on one pillar
* Strong commitment to one major goal

---

## Neji

Assign when:

* Elite performance in one pillar
* Goal progress exceeds 75%

---

## Kakashi

Assign when:

* Strong performance across all pillars
* No major weaknesses

---

## Luffy

Assign when:

* Completes a major long-term goal
* High Legacy Score
* Consistent goal pursuit

Important:
Luffy is NOT determined by streaks or completion percentage.

Luffy represents dream chasing.

---

## Minato

Assign when:

* Highest completion rates
* Strong performance across all pillars
* Rarely leaves tasks unfinished

Minato represents execution.

---

## Itachi

Assign when:

* Highest streaks
* Exceptional consistency
* Strong pillar maintenance

Itachi represents discipline.

---

# Endgame Philosophy

Luffy = Dream Chaser

Minato = Perfect Executor

Itachi = Ultimate Discipline

Luffy is below Minato and Itachi.

Minato and Itachi are equal-tier characters.

Minato wins through:

* Completion
* Efficiency
* Execution

Itachi wins through:

* Discipline
* Consistency
* Streaks

Users should naturally move between characters as their behavior changes.

Character images should be displayed whenever a character is assigned or changed.

Example:

Current Character:
⚡ Minato

Display:

* Character image
* Character name
* Character title
* Character description
* Why the user currently has this character
